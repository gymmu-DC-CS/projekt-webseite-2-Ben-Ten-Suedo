Reasons why you should buy the La Sportiva aequilibrium ST or LT gtx

Pro Aspects:

  • The shoes are made of lightweight and durable materials
  • They offer excellent grip and traction due to their thick Vibram outsole
  • They provide a comfortable fit for long hikes and are compatible with the most common crampons
  • Their Gore-Tex membrane makes them waterproof but breathable, which makes them perfect for every weather condition.
  • They are B2 hiking boots which means you can easily wear them for every summit under 4500 meter and over 4500 as approach gear.

Con Aspects:

  • The shoes come at a relatively high price point due to their advanced features and materials
  • Some users have reported that the sizing runs small, so trying them on before purchasing is very recommended!
  • The shoes may require a break in period to feel comfortable,
    so planning in enough time before you really tend to use them for is essential.

Two different Models

  • Aequilibrium ST GTX: This model is designed for technical approaches and mixed terrain, offering a balance of support and flexibility.
  • Aequilibrium LT GTX: This model is lighter and more agile, making it suitable for fast-paced hikes and less technical terrain.
