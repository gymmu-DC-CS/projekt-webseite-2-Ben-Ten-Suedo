Reasons why you should buy the La Sportiva aequilibrium ST or LT gtx

Pro Aspects:

  • The shoes are made of lightweight and durable materials
  • They offer excellent grip and traction due to their thick Vibram outsole
  • They provide a comfortable fit for long hikes and are compatible with the most common crampons
  • Their Gore-Tex membrane makes them waterproof but breathable, which makes them perfect for every weather condition.
  • They are B2 hiking boots which means you can easily wear them for every summit under 4500 meter and over 4500 as approach gear.

Con Aspects:

  • The shoes come at a relatively high price point due to their advanced features and materials
  • Some users have reported that the sizing runs small, so trying them on before purchasing is very recommended!
  • The shoes may require a break in period to feel comfortable, so planning in enough time before heavy use is essential.

Now that we talked about what makes those shoes so good lets see how they really look.

Two different Models

The LT model

The Aequilibrium LT GTX is designed for fast-paced hikes and less technical terrain, offering a lightweight and agile option for outdoor enthusiasts. The L in LT stands for leather, which means that the upper part of the shoe is made of leather, providing durability and support.

The ST model

The Aequilibrium ST GTX is designed for technical approaches and mixed terrain, offering a balance of support and flexibility. The S in ST stands for synthetic, which means that the upper part of the shoe is made of synthetic materials, providing a lightweight and breathable option for outdoor enthusiasts.
